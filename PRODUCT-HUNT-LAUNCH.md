## STEP 1: CREATE YOUR PRODUCT HUNT ACCOUNT (5 min)

1. Go to https://producthunt.com
2. Sign up with Google (use your Gmail)
3. Complete your profile with your photo and bio:
   > "Founder of CloserAI. Building AI tools to help real estate agents capture more leads 24/7. Always happy to chat with fellow makers."
4. Follow 20-30 popular makers in the SaaS space (builds your network)
5. Upvote 10-20 interesting products (algorithm likes active users)

**Wait at least 7 days before launching.** New accounts get flagged.

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

❌ **Don't launch on Monday or Friday** — Low traffic days
❌ **Don't spam other products' comments** — Gets you flagged
❌ **Don't use fake upvotes** — PH will ban your product
❌ **Don't ignore negative feedback** — Respond professionally
❌ **Don't have a broken demo** — Test everything day before
❌ **Don't forget to reply to comments** — Algorithm boosts active products
❌ **Don't launch with a new PH account** — Wait 7+ days after creation
