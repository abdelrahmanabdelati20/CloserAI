# SENDING STRATEGY

## For OLD Account (Recommended):
- Send **10-15 per day** for first week
- Gmail trusts aged accounts, so most land in inbox
- Spread throughout the day (don't send all at once)

## For NEW Account:
- Send **only 3-5 per day** for first 2 weeks
- Gmail watches new accounts for spam behavior
- Warm up by also: sending normal emails, receiving replies, marking messages as important
- After 2 weeks of warmup, you can scale to 10/day
- After 1 month, scale to 20/day

## Time to Send:
- **Best**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM recipient's local time
- **Good**: Monday 10 AM - 2 PM
- **Avoid**: Friday afternoons, weekends, holidays

## After Sending:
- Wait 3 days for reply
- Send follow-up email if no response
- After 3 follow-ups with no reply, move on

## Tracking:
Create a Google Sheet with columns:
- Date Sent | Company | Email | Status | Follow-up Date | Notes
