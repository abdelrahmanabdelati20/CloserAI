# CloserAI — Your Exact 7-Day Action Plan to Get First Clients

**Read this EVERY morning. Do EVERY task. Skip nothing.**

**Goal:** 3-5 paying clients in 30 days. First trial signup in 3 days. First paid client in 14 days.

---

## WHY THIS PLAN WORKS

Most founders fail because they do 20 things half-heartedly. This plan focuses on 3 channels and executes them DAILY:

1. **Cold Email** (you have 40+ drafts ready to send)
2. **LinkedIn** (1-on-1 personal outreach)
3. **Facebook Groups** (value-first posts)

Total time commitment: **2 hours/day**. That's it.

## DAY 1 (MONDAY) — LAUNCH DAY

### Morning (9:00 AM - 10:30 AM)

**[9:00 AM] Delete old RealtyFlow drafts (10 min)**
- Open Gmail
- Search: `in:drafts subject:"Your website visitors are leaving"`
- Select all → Delete
- Search: `in:drafts "REALTYFLOW"`
- Select all → Delete
- Empty trash

**[9:10 AM] Send your first 10 CloserAI drafts (30 min)**
- Open Gmail → Drafts
- Open each CloserAI HTML draft
- Quick review, then click **Send**
- Space them 3 minutes apart (don't send all at once)
- **Target: 10 emails sent by 9:40 AM**

**[9:40 AM] Track sends (5 min)**
- Create a Google Sheet called "CloserAI Outreach Tracker"
- Columns: Date | Contact | Email | Subject | Status | Follow-up Date
- Log every email you send

**[9:45 AM] Join LinkedIn groups (10 min)**
- Search for "Real Estate Agents" on LinkedIn
- Join 5 groups
- Also search "Real Estate Tech" and join 2 more

**[9:55 AM] Update your LinkedIn profile (15 min)**
- Add "Founder @ CloserAI" to your headline
- Write a short bio: "Building AI tools to help real estate agents capture 3x more leads 24/7"
- Add your profile photo
- Post your first status update (see template below)

**LinkedIn Status Update (copy-paste):**
```
Exciting update: I just launched CloserAI, an AI-powered chat assistant built specifically for real estate agents.

It captures leads 24/7, speaks 50+ languages, and scores them automatically so agents know who to call first.

```

### Afternoon (2:00 PM - 3:00 PM)

**[2:00 PM] Send 10 more emails (30 min)**
- Continue through your drafts
- Space 3 minutes apart
- **Total by end of day: 20 emails sent**

**[2:30 PM] Connect with 10 real estate agents on LinkedIn (20 min)**
- Search: "Real Estate Broker" + filter by city (try Miami first)
- Send 10 connection requests WITH a personal note:

```
Hi [NAME], I came across your profile and love your work at [THEIR COMPANY]. I'm building an AI tool for real estate agents and would love to connect with experienced brokers like you. No pitch — just want to learn from your experience!
```

**[2:50 PM] Engage on LinkedIn (10 min)**

- Like 10 posts from real estate agents
- Leave thoughtful comments on 3-5 posts

## WHAT TO DO WHEN YOU WANT TO QUIT

Every first-time founder hits the wall around Day 8-14. You'll feel like:
- "Nobody cares about my product"
- "I'm wasting my time"
- "The market is saturated"
- "I should just get a regular job"

**This is NORMAL. Every successful founder felt this.**

When it happens:

1. **Look at your tracker** — How many emails have you sent?
2. **If less than 500 emails total** — you haven't sent enough. Keep going.
3. **If more than 500 emails and zero results** — your messaging needs work. Try new angles.
4. **Take 24 hours off** — clear your head
5. **Come back with new energy**

**Reality check:** The average SaaS founder sends 1,000+ cold emails before their first paying client. You're not behind. You're just starting.
