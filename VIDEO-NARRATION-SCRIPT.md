# CloserAI Video Narration Script

**Total duration:** 2-3 minutes
**Slides:** 11 (CloserAI-Presentation.pptx)
**Recording tool:** Loom (free at loom.com) — use "Screen + Camera" mode

## How to Record

1. Open `CloserAI-Presentation.pptx` in PowerPoint or Google Slides (full screen)
2. Open Loom (browser extension or desktop app)
3. Select "Screen + Camera" (your face appears in bottom corner)
4. Click Record
5. Read the script below while advancing slides
6. Stop recording when done
7. Loom gives you a shareable link instantly

**Tips:**
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Smile — you're excited about your product
- Pause 1-2 seconds between slides
- Keep energy HIGH on slide 1 (the hook)
- Get serious on slide 2 (the problem)
- Get enthusiastic on slides 3-5 (the solution)
- Be matter-of-fact on slides 7-8 (comparison/pricing)
- End with urgency on slide 11 (CTA)

   - Upload to Facebook/Instagram as a video ad
   - Post on LinkedIn
   - Share in real estate Facebook groups
   - Include in your email signature

## Alternative: Convert Slides to Video

If you don't want to record with Loom, PowerPoint can export directly:

1. Open `CloserAI-Presentation.pptx` in PowerPoint
2. File → Export → Create a Video
3. Select "Full HD (1080p)"
4. Set timing: 5-8 seconds per slide (or use recorded timings)
5. Click "Create Video"

This creates a silent slideshow video. You can add background music using a free tool like Canva or CapCut.
