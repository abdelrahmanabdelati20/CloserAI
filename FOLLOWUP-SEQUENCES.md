## RESPONSE TIME RULES

**Golden rule:** Reply to every email within 2 hours during business hours.

- Within 15 min: Best chance of conversion
- Within 1 hour: Still very good
- Within 4 hours: Acceptable
- Within 24 hours: Minimum standard
- Longer than 24 hours: You're losing deals

Set up phone notifications for email. Every email is a potential $3,600+/year client.

## TRACKING FOLLOWUPS

Create a simple Google Sheet with these columns:

| Prospect Name | Email | Date Sent | Last Follow-Up | Next Follow-Up | Status | Notes |

**Statuses:**
- **New** — Just contacted
- **Followup 1** — Sent first follow-up
- **Followup 2** — Sent second follow-up
- **Replied** — Got a response
- **Demo Booked** — Meeting scheduled
- **Trial Started** — Signed up for trial
- **Paid** — Became a client
- **Lost** — No interest / not a fit
- **Revisit** — Try again in 3-6 months

Update this daily. It's the single most important sales habit.
