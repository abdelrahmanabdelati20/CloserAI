# CloserAI — Facebook Group Post Templates

## RULES FOR POSTING IN GROUPS

### DO:
- Read group rules before posting (avoid bans)
- Provide VALUE first, pitch last
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours
- Use your real name and profile
- Post during peak hours: Tue-Thu, 9-11 AM or 7-9 PM
- Engage with OTHER people's posts for 30 min before posting yours (algorithm boost)

### DON'T:
- Don't spam (1 post per group per week MAX)
- Don't post the same thing in 10 groups at once
- Don't mention your product in the first line
- Don't use excessive emojis
- Don't include links in the post (use "DM me" instead — algorithm hates external links)

### Best Groups to Post In:
1. **Lab Coat Agents** (157k members) — biggest real estate agent community
2. **Real Estate Referral Network** (300k+ members) — huge audience

4. **Real Estate Rockstars** — top-producer community
