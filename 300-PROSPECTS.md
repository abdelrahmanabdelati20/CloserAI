## About This List

This document contains **150+ vetted real estate brokerage prospects** across 41 US cities for the CloserAI AI chatbot product. Each prospect is an independent brokerage, boutique firm, or small-to-medium real estate team — the ideal buyers for an AI-powered lead-conversion chatbot.

- **Websites with their own domains** — they control their own sales process

## How to Use This List

### 1. Outreach Strategy
- **Personalize every email** — reference the city and, where available, the contact's first name
- **Send in small batches** (25-50/day max) to protect sender reputation
- **Follow-up cadence:** Day 1, Day 4, Day 10, Day 21
- **Best send times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM local time

### 2. Email Template Structure
Use the templates in `EMAIL-TEMPLATES.md`. Key subject line formats that work:
- "Quick question about [Brokerage Name]'s lead flow"
- "[First Name] — 30% more conversions for [City] agents?"
- "How [competitor] captured 40 leads overnight"

### 3. Tracking
Move each prospect through these stages in your CRM / Google Sheet:
`Not Contacted → Emailed → Opened → Replied → Demo Booked → Closed Won/Lost`

### 4. Compliance
- Every email must include a clear opt-out link (CAN-SPAM compliance)
- Never send to `info@` emails more than 2x without a response
- Respect replies asking to be removed

### 5. Conversion Targets
- Goal: **2-5% reply rate** on cold outreach
- Expected: ~3-7 demos from 150 prospects on first pass
- Close rate on demos: **20-30%** for warm demos
