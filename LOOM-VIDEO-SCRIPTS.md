# CloserAI — Loom Video Sales Scripts

**Video messages get 5-10x more replies than text emails.** Record these with Loom (free) and send them personally to prospects.

---

## WHY LOOM VIDEOS WORK:

1. **Personal** — recipient sees your face, hears your voice
2. **Shows effort** — they know you took time just for them
3. **Stands out** — they get 50 text emails but 0 video emails
4. **Shows the product** — you can screen-share the demo live
5. **Builds trust** — real person, not a bot

**Stats:** Loom claims 4x higher response rate, my experience says 5-10x for cold outreach.

---

## HOW TO RECORD YOUR VIDEOS:

1. **Download Loom**: https://loom.com (free — records up to 5 min videos)
2. **Install Chrome extension** or desktop app
3. **Record**: Click the Loom icon → "Screen + Camera" → Record
4. **Duration**: Keep it **under 90 seconds** (ideally 60 seconds)
5. **Background**: Clean, well-lit (face a window)
6. **Outfit**: Business casual (shirt or light blazer)
7. **Energy**: Be enthusiastic but not fake
8. **Script**: Know the script but don't READ it (sounds robotic)

## LOOM VIDEO BEST PRACTICES

### Thumbnail:
- **Smile!** Thumbnail is the first thing they see
- **Hold up your hand or wave** — attention-grabbing
- **Caption:** Loom auto-generates, but you can edit to something like "60 seconds for [THEIR NAME]"

### Video Title:
- "Quick 60-sec message for [NAME]" 
- "Personal video for [THEIR BUSINESS NAME]"
- "[THEIR NAME] — saw your website, quick thought"

### Email Subject (When Sending):
- "Quick video for you ([THEIR NAME])"
- "[YOUR NAME] sent you a video"
- "60 seconds just for you"
- "Saw your website — made you a video"

### In the Email Body:
Keep it ULTRA short:

> "Hey [NAME],
> 
> I recorded a quick 60-second video for you showing something I noticed about your website.
> 
> [LOOM THUMBNAIL WITH LINK]
> 
> Worth a look!
> 
> [YOUR NAME]"

That's it. The video does the work.

## EQUIPMENT SETUP (One-Time)

### Free Setup:
- **Built-in laptop camera** (fine for starting)
- **Built-in mic** (fine but not great)
- **Natural light from a window** (face toward it)
- **Clean background** (plain wall)

### $50 Upgrade (Optional):
- **Logitech C920** webcam — much better quality
- **Simple USB lapel mic** — clearer audio
- **Ring light** — professional lighting

### Recording Tips:
1. **Test first** — record 10 seconds and watch it back
2. **Smile while talking** — even if faking it, changes your tone
3. **Look at the camera** — not the screen (practice this)
4. **Don't read** — memorize key points, speak naturally
5. **Edit if needed** — Loom has simple trim tools
6. **Add captions** — Loom auto-generates, big impact

## WHAT TO SAY IF YOU'RE NERVOUS

First 10 videos will feel awkward. Everyone's first videos suck. That's fine.

Things to remember:
- **They can't judge you** — they're just watching their inbox
- **It's just 60 seconds** — even bad videos are under a minute
- **Imperfect beats perfect** — a real human message beats a polished corporate one
- **You're helping them** — not bothering them

Just hit record. The more you do, the better you get.

**Your first video will be your worst. Your 20th will be solid. Your 100th will be incredible.**
