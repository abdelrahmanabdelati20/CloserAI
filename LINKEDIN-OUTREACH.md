# CloserAI — LinkedIn Outreach Playbook

Complete LinkedIn strategy with scripts, search strategies, and daily routine.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS PLAYBOOK

1. Open LinkedIn.com and log in
2. Use the search strategies below to find prospects
3. Send 10-15 connection requests per day (LinkedIn limit — don't exceed)
4. When they accept, send the follow-up message
5. Track everything in a spreadsheet

**IMPORTANT**: LinkedIn heavily restricts bulk messaging. Send slowly and personalize each one.

---

## SEARCH STRATEGIES (Find 100+ Prospects Per Day)

### Strategy 1: Job Title Search
In LinkedIn search, type these (one at a time):
- "Real Estate Broker" + [City name]
- "Real Estate Agent" + [City name]
- "Team Lead" + "Real Estate" + [City]
- "Broker Owner" + "Real Estate"
- "Managing Broker"
- "Real Estate Entrepreneur"

Filter by:
- **Location**: Your target city
- **Current company**: Real estate brokerages
- **Connections**: 2nd degree (can send connection request)

### Strategy 2: Company Search
Search for these company names, then click "People" to see all employees:
- Compass
- Keller Williams
- RE/MAX
- Coldwell Banker
- Sotheby's International Realty
- Douglas Elliman
- Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices
- eXp Realty
- Century 21

### Strategy 3: Hashtag Search
Search LinkedIn for these hashtags and connect with active posters:
- #realestateagent
- #realtor
- #luxuryrealestate
- #realestateteam
- #realestatebroker

### Strategy 4: Real Estate Groups
Join these LinkedIn groups (free) and message active members:
- "Real Estate Professionals"
- "Real Estate Agents & Brokers"
- "Luxury Real Estate Network"
- "Inman Connect"
- "NAR (National Association of REALTORS)"

## DAILY LINKEDIN ROUTINE (1 hour per day)

**Morning (30 min):**
1. Search for 20 new prospects using Strategy 1-4 above
2. Send 10-15 connection requests with Template 1 or 4
3. Accept any new connections

**Afternoon (30 min):**
1. Send follow-up Message 1 to anyone who accepted today
2. Send Message 2 to prospects from 3 days ago
3. Send Message 3 to prospects from 7 days ago
4. Engage with 5-10 posts from real estate agents (comment thoughtfully)

**Expected results after 30 days:**
- 300+ new connections
- 50+ conversations started
- 10-15 demo calls booked
- 3-5 paying clients

## LINKEDIN DO's AND DON'Ts

### DO:
- Personalize every message with their name and something about their business
- Send voice notes occasionally
- Engage with their posts before pitching
- Post valuable content 3x per week
- Track responses in a spreadsheet

### DON'T:
- Send more than 15 connection requests per day (LinkedIn will restrict you)
- Copy-paste the same message to everyone
- Pitch in the first message
- Send spam or mass messages
- Buy LinkedIn lead lists (they're garbage)
